About DigiSign

Pioneering the future of digital document signing

Our Story

Founded in 2015, DigiSign was born out of frustration with the inefficiencies of paper-based signatures. Our team of legal tech experts and software engineers set out to create a solution that would make document signing as simple as sending an email.

Today, DigiSign powers over 10 million transactions annually for businesses ranging from solo entrepreneurs to Fortune 500 companies. We're proud to be recognized as a leader in digital signature technology, with awards from TechCrunch, Forbes, and the LegalTech Association.

Our Mission

At DigiSign, we believe that paperwork should never stand in the way of progress. Our mission is to eliminate the friction in document signing by providing:

  • The most secure digital signature platform available
  • Intuitive tools that anyone can use
  • Legally binding solutions that stand up in court
  • Seamless integrations with your existing workflow

We're committed to continuous innovation, ensuring our customers always have access to the most advanced document signing technology available.
